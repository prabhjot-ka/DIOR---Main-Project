The sole objective of perfume is to add an
appealing scent to the bodies of people,
animals, food, objects, and living places. It
is a combine of aromatic essential oils or
aroma compounds, fixatives, and solvents,
usually in liquid form. Ingredients that
generate and spread a pleasant and aromatic
odor are referred to as perfumes. They are
made up of synthetic blends of essential oils
and aromatic compounds. "Right from the earliest
days of theoretical science up to the modern era,
perfumes have considerably assisted in the
progress of the field of organic chemistry as
concerns methods, methodical classification.
